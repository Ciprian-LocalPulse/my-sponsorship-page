# Public Edition Notice

My Sponsorship Page is published here as a Public Edition for sponsorship discovery, public trust positioning, supporter conversion, portfolio review, and commercial inquiry routing.

## Purpose of the Public Edition

The Public Edition is intended to communicate:

- public sponsorship positioning
- premium trust and credibility signals
- public mission and support framing
- sponsor conversion potential
- public governance posture
- sponsor-safe and enterprise-safe evaluation material

## What the Public Edition May Include

The public repository may include:

- README and public sponsor messaging
- static sponsorship page or public interface preview
- public sponsor tier presentation intentionally published in this repository
- governance documents
- safe support and security reporting instructions
- high-level commercial evaluation language

## What the Public Edition Does Not Include

The Public Edition does not include and should not be used to infer:

- private sponsor records, donor information, transaction records, invoices, or payment metadata
- private sponsor tier fulfillment details, private benefits delivery, or supporter account information
- payment provider configuration, webhooks, API keys, credentials, secrets, or environment variables
- private analytics, conversion data, campaign performance data, or supporter segmentation
- unreleased brand assets, private messaging drafts, launch materials, or sponsor communications
- private prompts, internal playbooks, pricing strategy, partnership terms, or commercial negotiation details

## Private Sponsorship and Commercial Materials

Private sponsorship and commercial materials, if made available, are separate from this public repository and may only be accessed through an authorized sponsorship relationship, private channel, or written agreement.

Private sponsorship discussions should remain private and should not be opened as public GitHub issues when they involve pricing, private benefits, donor records, payment details, fulfillment details, sponsor communications, partnership terms, or confidential evaluation criteria.

## Responsible Use

My Sponsorship Page is positioned for lawful sponsorship discovery, public trust positioning, supporter conversion, and commercial inquiry routing. Public materials must not be used to support credential misuse, payment abuse, donor-data exposure, impersonation, deceptive fundraising, or activity outside a lawful and authorized context.

## Public Communication Standard

Issues, pull requests, discussions, and references to this repository should remain calm, factual, sponsor-safe, enterprise-safe, and trust-preserving. Do not publish sensitive findings, private sponsor information, or private implementation assumptions in public channels.
