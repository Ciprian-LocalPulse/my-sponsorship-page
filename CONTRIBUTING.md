# Contributing Policy

Thank you for your interest in My Sponsorship Page. This repository is maintained as a controlled Public Edition and premium sponsorship/trust-positioning surface.

## Contribution Model

External code contributions are not accepted unless explicitly approved by the repository owner in advance.

This restriction protects the Public Edition and prevents accidental disclosure of private sponsor information, commercial materials, brand strategy, donor records, payment details, analytics, supporter segmentation, or security-sensitive information.

## Contributions That May Be Considered

The maintainer may selectively consider non-sensitive contributions such as:

- typo fixes or documentation clarity improvements
- governance file improvements
- accessibility notes for public documentation
- issue reports about broken links or inaccurate public claims
- safe suggestions for public repository hygiene

Please open an issue before preparing any pull request. Unapproved pull requests may be closed without review.

## Contributions That Will Not Be Accepted

Do not submit, request, or describe:

- changes to design, visuals, sponsor tier structure, branding, assets, or README messaging unless explicitly requested by the maintainer
- private sponsor records, donor information, transaction records, invoices, or payment metadata
- sponsor tier fulfillment details, private benefits delivery, or supporter account information
- payment provider configuration, webhooks, API keys, credentials, environment variables, or secrets
- private analytics, conversion data, campaign performance data, or supporter segmentation
- unreleased brand assets, private messaging drafts, launch materials, or sponsor communications
- private prompts, internal playbooks, pricing strategy, partnership terms, or commercial negotiation material
- changes that enable deceptive fundraising, credential misuse, payment abuse, donor-data exposure, impersonation, or extraction of private materials

## Public Edition Boundary

The Public Edition communicates safe sponsorship positioning, premium trust posture, public mission framing, governance maturity, and supporter conversion value. It is not a private sponsor portal and does not accept contributions that attempt to recreate private supporter workflows, private benefits, or commercial implementation details.

## Pull Request Expectations

If the maintainer explicitly invites a contribution:

1. Keep the change narrow and documentation-focused unless otherwise approved.
2. Do not modify design, visuals, sponsor tier structure, branding, assets, README messaging, or private implementation details unless specifically requested by the maintainer.
3. Avoid adding dependencies, scripts, data collection, analytics, payment logic, sponsorship fulfillment logic, or executable behavior.
4. Keep language calm, enterprise-safe, trust-preserving, donor-safe, and legally careful.
5. Confirm that no secrets, credentials, donor data, sponsor records, payment details, private assets, or private methods are included.

## Issues

Public issues should be limited to non-sensitive repository concerns. Do not open public issues containing vulnerability details, sponsor records, donor information, payment details, private commercial details, brand strategy, implementation-sensitive details, or commercial negotiation material. Use SECURITY.md for security reporting.

## License and Ownership

The public repository materials are governed by the applicable license files in this repository. Private sponsor records, private commercial materials, paid deliverables, supporter data, and non-public assets are not included in the Public Edition.

## Maintainer Discretion

The maintainer may close issues or pull requests that are out of scope, unsafe, duplicative, commercially sensitive, privacy-sensitive, legally sensitive, or inconsistent with the premium sponsorship and trust positioning of this repository.
