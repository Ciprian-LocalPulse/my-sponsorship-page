# Public Edition Notice

The my-sponsorship-page repository is published as a Public Edition sponsorship and governance surface for LocalPulse-related work.

It is intentionally limited to public-safe sponsorship content, static assets, contact routes, and governance documentation. It is not a complete product repository, enterprise transfer package, diligence room, or production implementation.

## Included in the Public Edition

The Public Edition may include:

- Static sponsorship page files.
- Public image assets.
- Public README and governance documentation.
- High-level capability and research descriptions.
- Public sponsorship, support, security, contribution, and licensing boundaries.

## Excluded from the Public Edition

The Public Edition excludes:

- Private source logic and proprietary implementation details.
- Customer data, client environments, and deployment assumptions.
- Operational deception logic, detection rules, telemetry pipelines, or monitoring schemas.
- Internal prompts, private playbooks, response procedures, or business strategy.
- API keys, tokens, credentials, secrets, or private configuration.
- Commercial contracts, pricing, acquisition materials, and enterprise transfer records.

## Private Enterprise Edition

A Private Enterprise Edition or transfer package may be prepared separately for qualified commercial, acquisition, or diligence conversations.

That package may include private materials under written agreement only. Nothing in the Public Edition grants access to those materials or creates a right to use them.

## Sponsor Materials

Sponsor-only materials, if offered, are separate from the Public Edition and are governed by the applicable platform terms or written agreement. Sponsorship does not automatically authorize redistribution, resale, private transfer, or enterprise use of non-public materials.

## Procurement-Safe Interpretation

The Public Edition is suitable for initial review of public positioning and governance posture. Any production, compliance, security, transfer, or acquisition assessment should be performed against the applicable private package and written commercial terms.
